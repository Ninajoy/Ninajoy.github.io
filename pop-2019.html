Quests for a Front End Developer

In november 2018 i fell into a depression. I was numbed, anxious, had an immense lack of selfworth and could not stop crying. It was hard. Acceptance from myself and others were one of the keys to start my recovery. The other keys were all my own to find and use: Going to a psychologist, starting medication, keeping to a daily rhythm, mindfullness, yoga and hapto therapy. I am still recovering with the help of all these keys and it is my goal to be fully recovered in 2019.
The only constant in life is change. I want that change in my life to be renewal and innovation I want to keep growing towards the best version I can be. I believe that the following technical and social skills will help me innovate myself and the company I work for.

  • Get certified: Responisve webdesign & Javascript Algorithms And Data Structures Certification @FreeCodeCamp
  • Get updated: Visit Front End congresses to learn about new developments and read FE newssites very week
  • Participate in innovations: Participate in innovative projects (within the team) & update my Tshape with Unit Testing, Accessibility & Security skills
    Personal skills:
